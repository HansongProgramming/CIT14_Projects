A TRIBUTE TO AUGNINA KRIZEL REBURIANO:

Augnina, a complex mixture of both beauty and chaos

She's a film, but not like any other film
To find her is simple.
You just have to find me
And she will be there
She will be there
She will always, be there
        Augnina, is not a typical woman, 
        I often think of her as a film. 
        One with unpredictability, with joy,
        sorrow, hope, dread, anguish, and solitude.

        On another day, i'd think of her as a daisy,
        symbolizing change, joy, and new beginnings.
        She's my daisy, strangely found;
        in the field of roses.

MINIMATOOLS

By: Hans Flores

Minimatools Overview:

            Minimatools is a Clip Studio Paint
            Extension with the goal of 
            simplifying workflows, by 
            integrating an on screen shortcut
            manager, efficiently cutting 
            the time to memorize and manually
            reach for utilities across 
            the screen by half

Minimatools description:

            Created on the 7th of December 2022 
            By Flores Hans, Who suddenly got 
            irritated with having to press 
            buttons from accross the screen.
            Allowing the erection of the
            small widget called 
            MINIMATOOLS.

Minimatools Price:

        Minimatools is a free and
        open source Extension for

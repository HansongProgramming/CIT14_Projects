AI:BOU-Overview

AI:BOU was initially named AI:BUD which aims to be a virtual buddy to aid people suffering from Mental Illnesses or Substance abuse.

However it would be limited to people with access to internet and has technological gadgets, therefore we scratched this idea and decided to create an affordable Gadget instead.

AI:BOU-Targets

AI:BOU is erected from the desire to make life easier for those who can’t catch up with society, and provide maintenance for those with capabilities.

To make sure that AI:BOU serves as the ultimate partner for humanity, we also changed it’s power source and over all casing. By using Solar Panels we can now charge AI:BOU Without spending or wasting electricity, And by using Recycled Plastic as it’s casing
